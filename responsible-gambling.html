Responsible Gambling

Keep Play Controlled

Play should remain optional, contained, and affordable. If it stops feeling that way, step back immediately and use support tools before continuing.

Set Limits

  • Choose a fixed budget before you play.
  • Do not treat losses as something to chase.
  • Take regular breaks away from the screen.

Watch the Signs

  • Hiding play from others
  • Using borrowed money
  • Feeling unable to stop

Take Action

If play feels compulsive, pause your account activity, avoid new deposits, and seek local responsible gambling support in your region.
